University Policies

Academic Policy

If you wish to transfer courses in our school, you are allowed to pay for 2 exemptions and 3 transfers or vice versa (that is 3 exemptions and 2 transfers) per semester at the rate of K30,000 (ZMW 300) per course per semester.

Fees Policy

No fees no classes. Two instalments are allowed. Pay into the account provided on this form. Please check with your country office on the local currency equivalents.

Graduation Policy

All graduating students must complete their prescribed number of courses and clear with the academic dean as well as pay all fees and then receive the invitation to graduate after clearance. Management of the University and its partners reserve the right of admission to graduation.
